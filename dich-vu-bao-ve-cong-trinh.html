    Gioi thieu ve Dich vu Bao ve Cong trinh, Nha may, Kho bai

    Cac cong trinh xay dung, nha may san xuat va kho bai la nhung muc tieu co gia tri tai san lon va tinh chat rieng biet. Day cung la nhung noi thuong xuyen xay ra that thoat vat tu, nhap nhang hang hoa, mat cap thiet bi hoac va cham gio gian lao dong. Mot doi ngu bao vey chuyen nghiep co the dam bao moi quy trinh duoc kiem soat chat che, han che toi thieu thiet hai tai san.

    TVS Giai Phap Xanh cung cap dich vu bao ve cong trinh toan dien cho nhieu loai hinh muc tieu co dinh. Chung toi hieu ro moi cong trinh, moi nha may deu co cac dac thu rieng ve mat layout, quy trinh san xuat va luong hang hoa ra vao. Do vay, moi phuong an bao ve duoc ca nhan hoa dua tren khao sat thuc te, dam bao hieu qua toi uu nhat.

    Voi hon 15 nam kinh nghiem trong linh vuc an ninh cong trinh, TVS da trien khai hon 156 hop dong bao ve thanh cong cho cac doanh nghiep trong nuoc va nuoc ngoai, tu cac nha may san xuat linh kien dien tu tai KCN Tan Binh cho den cac kho bai logistics tai Cat Lai va cum cong trinh xay dung cao tang tai trung tam TP.HCM.

    "TVS giai quyet triet de nhu cau bao ve nha may cua chung toi. He thong tuan tra chip va kiem soat container giam thieu that thoat vat tu dang ke. Sau 2 nam hop tac, that thoat nguyen vat lieu giam 85%." - Giam doc San xuat, Cong ty ABC Precision

    Cac Goi Giai Phap An Ninh Cong Trinh, Nha May, Kho Bai

    Chung toi cung cap 3 goi dich vu an ninh cong trinh co dinh, duoc thiet ke phu hop voi tung loai hinh va muc do nhu cau cua khach hang doanh nghiep:

    1. Bao ve Nha may San xuat

    Giai phap an ninh toan dien cho nha may san xuat, dam bao quy trinh san xuat lien tuc, an toan. TVS bo tri nhan vien bao ve tai cac vi tri chot cua nha may, kiem soat nghiem nga nhan su ra vao, giam sat viec xuat nhap nguyen vat lieu va thanh pham.

    • Kiem soat ra vao nha may: Kiem tra toan bo nguoi va phuong tien ra vao theo danh sach duoc uy quyen, cap the tu ra vao cho khach hang va doi tac.
    • Giam sat xuat nhap hang hoa: Kiem tra va doi chieu chung tu xuat nhap kho, kiem soat san luong container, bao cao lech thuc te so voi chung tu.
    • Tuan tra chip dinh danh: Nhan vien bao ve tuan tra theo linh trinh da lap trinh, bam chip tai cac diem kiem soat, du lieu duoc dong bo ve trung tam giam sat.
    • Phong chay chua chay: Kiem tra va bao duong he thong PCCC hang ngay, dien tap phuong an chuong trinh, bao cao nhung diem nguy co chay no.
    • Giam sat camera: Camera an ninh duoc ket noi voi trung tam giam sat 24/7, nhan vien giam sat phat hien va xu ly bat thuong kip thoi.

    2. Bao ve Kho bai, Bai vat tu

    Giai phap an ninh cho kho bai logistics, bai vat tu xay dung, bai container, dam bao kiem soat hang hoa ra vao, chong that thoat va hu hong hang hoa.

    • Kiem soat container va phuong tien van tai: Kiem tra niem phong container, doi chieu so container voi van don, kiem soat xe tai ra vao.
    • Quan ly khoa niem: Kiem tra va ban giao niem khoa, niem phong kho hang vao dau va cuoi moi ca truc.
    • Tuan tra bao ve chu vi: Tuan tra dinh ky doc theo hang rao chu vi, phat hien cac diem xam nhap, hu hong hang rao.
    • Kiem soat thoat nuoc va PCCC: Dam bao he thong phong chay chua chay hoat dong tot, phat hien su co cham chay hoac troi nuoc.

    3. Bao ve Cong trinh Xay dung

    Giam thieu that thoat vat tu xay dung, nhan cong la dong va dam bao an toan tai cong truong trong va ngoai gio lam viec.

    • Tuan tra ban dem: Tuan suat tuan tra ban dem tang cuong, kiem tra kho vat tu, khu vuc de xe may thiet bi, may moc thi cong.
    • Kiem soat vat tu xay dung: Kiem tra kholuong vat tu dua vao cong trinh va vat tu dua ra khoi cong trinh, doi chieu voi phieu xuat nhap kho.
    • Chong dot nhap va trom cap: Chot truc 24/7 tai cong chinh, kiem soat nguoi la, phoi hop voi cong an dia phuong xu ly dot nhap.
    • An toan PCCC: Kiem tra cac thiet bi PCCC tai cong truong, canh bao nguy co chay no tu cac qua trinh han cat, dien.

    Kiem soat That thoat Tai san, Vat tu Xay dung

    That thoat tai san la van de lon nhat cua cac chu dau tu, chu doanh nghiep khi thue bao ve cong trinh. TVS ap dung quy trinh kiem soat that thoat 3 lop:

    Lop 1: Kiem soat Ra vao

    • 100% phuong tien ra vao duoc kiem tra: Xe tai, container, xe may xay dung deu phai dung lai de kiem tra khong gian hang, thung xe va cac vat dung ca nhan.
    • So sanh chung tu xuat nhap: Moi luot xuat hang ra khoi cong trinh deu phai co phieu xuat kho hop le, so luong thuc te duoc kiem tra va doi chieu.
    • Camera ghi nhan bien so: Camera ghi hinh bien so xe ra vao duoc luu tru it nhat 30 ngay de phuc vu tra cuu khi can.

    Lop 2: Kiem ke Dinh ky

    • Kiem ke hang ngay: Nhan vien bao ve kiem ke vat tu, tai san tren cong trinh vao dau va cuoi moi ca truc, ghi nhan su thay doi.
    • Ban giao ca chi tiet: Cuoi moi ca, nhan vien ban giao lai hien trang tai san, vat tu va ky nhan bang bien ban dien tu.
    • Bao cao dac biet: Phat hien mat mat hoac hu hong tai san, bao cao ngay cho giam sat vien va khach hang trong vong 30 phut.

    Lop 3: Kiem toan Dot xuat

    • Kiem toan bat ngo: Giam sat vien TVS thuc hien kiem toan dot xuat tai cong trinh it nhat 2 lan/tuan vao cac khung gio khac nhau.
    • Phat hien bat thuong: Doi ngũ kiem toan co the phat hien cac sai lech giau thong ke, phat hien cac vu mat cap chua duoc bao cao.
    • Bao cao kiem toan: Ket qua kiem toan duoc bao cao cho khach hang hang tuan, dam bao tinh minh bach va kip thoi xu ly.

    Kiem soat Luong xe Ra vao, Xuat nhap Hang hoa (Logistics Security)

    Logistics security la mot trong nhung the manh cua TVS, dac biet doi voi cac kho bai, bai container va khu cong nghe cao. Luong xe ra vao can duoc kiem soat chat che de tranh that thoat hang hoa, bun lung hoac xuat nhap trai phep.

    • Quy trinh kiem soat xe: Dung xe tai tram kiem soat, kiem tra giay to xe, doi chieu hang hoa voi van don, kiem tra niem phong container, chup hinh luu hinh anh xe.
    • Kiem tra container: Kiem tra tinh nguyen ven cua niem phong container, doi chieu so niem phong, so container voi chung tu xuat nhap khau.
    • He thong camera bien so: Camera ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) ghi nhan toan bo xe ra vao, tu dong canh bao neu so xe nam trong danh sach nghi ngo.
    • Kiem tra nguoi giao nhan: Xac thuc danh tinh nguoi giao nhan hang, kiem tra giay gioi thieu cua cong ty van tai truoc khi cho phep ra vao.
    • Quy trinh xuat nhap ban dem: Xuat nhap duoc giam sat chat che hoan, quay phim toan bo qua trinh boc xep, co nhan vien bao ve truc tiep giam sat.
    "TVS trien khai hoc thuyet kiem soat container va logistics security cho kho bai Cat Lai cua chung toi. Luong container thoat ra ngoai trai phep giaonhu khong con. Bao cao du lieu ra vao hang ngay giup chung toi quan ly hieu qua hon." - Giam doc Logistics, Cong ty XNK Viet Phat

    Hệ Thong Tuan Tra Ban Dem Chong Dot Nhap & Phong Chong Chay No (PCCC)

    Ban dem la thoi diem cac doi tuong xam nhap hoat dong nhieu nhat, dong thoi cung la luc nguy co chay no de xay ra nhat (do su co dien, cham dieu, hoac co y). TVS thiet ke he thong tuan tra dem chuyen biet cho cac muc tieu cong trinh:

    • Tuan suat tuan tra: Tuan tra 30-45 phut/lan trong ca dem, bam chip tu dong tai cac diem kiem soat (toi thieu 12 diem/cong trinh).
    • Kiem tra niem phong: Kiem tra tinh nguyen ven cua niem phong container, niem phong cua van, niem phong tai dien vao cuoi moi ca dem.
    • Kiem tra PCCC: Kiem tra thiet bi bao chay, binh chua chay, he thong chua chay tu dong, den bao su co, loi thoat hiem moi dem.
    • Ung cuu nhanh: Doi ung cuu TVS san sang huy dong 15 phut khi co bao dong tu he thong camera hoac bao chay.
    • GPS tracking: Thiet bi dinh vi GPS duoc lap tren nguoi nhan vien bao ve, kiem soat thuc dia tuyen di tuan tra, bao cao tinh trang tre hoac bo sot diem.

    Quy trinh Kiem soat An ninh Nghiem ngat

    TVS ap dung quy trinh kiem soat an ninh 5 buoc tieu chuan cho tat ca cac muc tieu cong trinh, nha may va kho bai:

    1. Khao sat danh gia ruii ro: TVS cư khao sat thuc dia, danh gia layout, xac dinh diem yeu, diem mù, lo trinh tuan tra, so luong nhan vien toi uu.
    2. Thiet ke phuong an: Xay dung phuong an bao ve chi tiet: so do bo tri nhan su, so do tuan tra, quy trinh kiem soat ra vao, phuong an PCCC.
    3. Trien khai nhan su: Tuyen chon va tap huan nhan su bao ve theo phuong an, dam bao nhan vien thong thao quy trinh PCCC, su dung camera, phan mem bao cao.
    4. Giam sat van hanh: Giam sat vien co mat tai hien truong hoac giam sat tu xa 24/7, bao cao cap nhat tinh hinh hang ca.
    5. Danh gia va cai tien: Huy dong danh gia hang quy, cai tien phuong an, cai tien quy trinh dua tren du lieu bao cao thuc te.

    Tai sao chon TVS Bao ve Cong trinh, Nha may, Kho bai?

    • Kinh nghiem chuyen sau: Hon 15 nam, hon 156 hop dong, tu nha may san xuat den kho bai, cong trinh xay dung cao tang va cum cong trinh logistics.
    • He thong tuan tra chip: Toan bo nhan vien tuan tra bang bam chip tai diem — du lieu duoc dong bo cloud, ban quan ly co the kiem tra online bat ky luc nao.
    • Giam sat tu xa 24/7: Camera an ninh, bao chay, GPS tracking nhan vien duoc ket noi voi trung tam giam sat TVS hoat dong 24/7.
    • Bao cao minh bach: Bao cao ca dien tu, hinh anh kiem tra, bang kiem niem phong, video giam sat duoc luu tru it nhat 30 ngay.
    • Dam bao PCCC chuyen nghiep: Nhan vien bao ve deu co chung chi PCCC co ban, dien tap dinh ky, kiem tra thiet bi PCCC moi ngay.
    • Bao hiem trach nhiem dan su: Toan bo dich vu duoc bao hiem day du, cam ket den bu khi xay ra thiet hai do loi cua nhan vien TVS.
    Cam Ket Den Bu 200% Neu That Thoat Tai San

    TVS cam ket den bu toi thieu 200% gia tri tai san neu xay ra mat mat, hu hong tai san do loi bat can cua nhan vien bao ve TVS. Dieu khoan duoc ghi ro trong hop dong dich vu.

    Goi ngay de nhan hop dong bao ve

    Cau hoi Thuong gap ve Bao ve Cong trinh, Nha may, Kho bai

    TVS co den bu khi xay ra that thoat vat tu khong?

    Co. TVS cam ket den bu toi thieu 200% gia tri tai san neu xay ra mat mat, hu hong tai san do loi bat can cua nhan vien bao ve TVS. Dieu khoan duoc ghi cu the trong hop dong truoc khi trien khai. Dieu nay dam bao quyen loi toi da cho khach hang va tao ap luc trach nhiem cho nhan vien bao ve.

    Nhan vien bao ve cong trinh cua TVS co chung chi PCCC khong?

    Co. 100% nhan vien bao ve cong trinh cua TVS deu duoc dao tao va co chung chi PCCC co ban. Ngoai ra, TVS con cung cap dao tao bo sung dinh ky (6 thang/lan) ve phong chong chay no cho toan bo nhan vien. Doi ngu con duoc dien tap xu ly tinh huong chay no thuc te tai cong trinh it nhat 1 lan/quy.

    Quy trinh kiem soat container ra vao the nao?

    Quy trinh kiem soat container tieu chuan cua TVS bao gom: (1) Kiem tra giay to xe va nguoi lai, (2) Doi chieu so container voi van don, (3) Kiem tra tinh nguyen ven niem phong container, (4) Chup anh container va bien so xe, (5) Ghi nhan hinh anh vao phan mem bao cao. Toan bo quy trinh duoc giam sat boi camera hien truong va camera giam sat tu xa cua trung tam TVS.

    Co the giam thieu so luong nhan su bao ve vao ngay thu bay, chu nhat khong?

    Co. TVS thiet ke phuong an bao ve linh hoat, co the giam bot nhan su vao cuoi tuan neu cong trinh khong co hoat dong san xuat. Tuy nhien, vẫn dam bao it nhat 1 nhan vien bao ve truc 24/7 tai moi cong trinh de kiem soat ra vao va xu ly tinh huong khan cap.

    TVS co ho tro tuan tra bang xe may (mobile patrol) cho nhieu diem bao ve khong?

    Co. TVS co dich vu tuan tra co dong (mobile patrol) cho khach hang co nhieu diem bao ve rieng le hoac can giam sat dinh ky nhieu vi tri. Doi tuan tra bang xe may co dinh vi GPS, trang bi camera hanh trinh va lien lac truc tiep voi trung tam dieu khien. Tuan suat tuan tra tu 1-4 lan/dem tuy theo nhu cau khach hang.

    Thoi gian trien khai hop dong bao ve cong trinh la bao lau?

    TVS co the trien khai trong vong 24-48 gio sau khi ky hop dong. Quy trinh: khao sat thuc dia, thiet ke phuong an, tuyen chon nhan su, tap huan tai hien truong va trien khai chinh thuc. Doi voi cac hop dong khan cap (co the trong vai gio), TVS san sang trien khai nhan su tu nguon nhan su du bi co san.

    Can giai phap an ninh cho cong trinh, nha may, kho bai cua ban?

    Goi ngay hotline — tu van mien phi, khao sat thuc te, gui bao gia chi tiet trong 2 gio. Ho tro 24/7 ke ca thu bay, chu nhat va ngay le.
